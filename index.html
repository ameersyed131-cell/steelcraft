// OUR STORY

BUILT ON STEEL, DRIVEN BY PASSION

SteelCraft Industries was born from a simple belief: that Indian hospitality deserves world-class kitchen infrastructure. Founded in 2005 by a team of engineers with deep expertise in metallurgy and food service design, we set out to bridge the gap between imported equipment quality and local manufacturing capability.

Today, our state-of-the-art facility in Balanagar houses CNC laser cutters, automated bending machines, and a dedicated TIG welding bay. Every piece of equipment undergoes rigorous multi-stage quality checks before leaving our facility.

Our client list reads like a who's who of Indian hospitality — from luxury hotels to hospital kitchens, from cloud kitchen operators to institutional caterers. Each project strengthens our resolve to deliver nothing less than perfection.
